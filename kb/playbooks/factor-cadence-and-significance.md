# Factor Cadence And Significance

This dashboard should not update everything at the same speed.

## Intraday / near-live

- government bond yields
- curve slope
- equity index leadership
- stock prices
- volume and liquidity
- breaking events

These matter because they explain what is moving now.

## Event-driven

- central bank communication
- inflation prints
- payrolls / jobs
- RBI / Fed decisions
- auctions, sanctions, tariffs, wars, major deals

These matter because they can change the regime quickly.

## Daily / weekly refresh

- cross-stock relation graph
- sector linkage notes
- company network context
- paper-driven factor overlays

These matter because they shape interpretation, not just the latest tick.

## Decision-support rule

Facts should refresh at their natural cadence.

Interpretation should reuse cached facts whenever possible.

The dashboard should make it clear:

- what is truly live
- what is cached but still relevant
- what is interpretation layered on top
