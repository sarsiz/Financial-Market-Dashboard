# Event Interpretation Playbook

- Ask in order: what changed, why now, is it macro or idiosyncratic, who benefits, who is exposed, and what confirms or invalidates the move.
- Escalate geopolitical, tariff, sanctions, central-bank, and inflation surprises first because they usually hit bonds before stocks.
- Translate events into market channels: yields, FX, commodities, liquidity, valuation, margins, and demand.
